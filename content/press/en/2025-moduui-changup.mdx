**SparkLabs** has been selected as an operating agency for the **'Startup for All'** program hosted by Korea's Ministry of SMEs and Startups. Building on a track record of 320+ portfolio investments, SparkLabs will lead discovery and acceleration of early-stage founders.

## Program overview

'Startup for All' is a government-backed program for technology and knowledge-based early-stage founders, providing commercialization funding, mentorship, and networking opportunities.

## SparkLabs' distinctive role

SparkLabs brings a differentiated value proposition to the program:

- **Global expansion support**: Connection to six continents through the SparkLabs network
- **AI-First focus**: Specialized investment and mentorship for AI and deep-tech startups
- **Operator-led mentorship**: Practical advice from partners who have been founders themselves
