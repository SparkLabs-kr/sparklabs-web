**SparkLabs Partners** has officially opened its Gyeongsangbuk-do branch inside Handong Global University. The opening follows a four-party MOU between SparkLabs Partners, Gyeongsangbuk-do Province, Pohang TechnoPark, and Semyung Christian Hospital.

## Why Pohang

SparkLabs Partners selected Pohang as a strategic regional base for building a locally anchored bio & healthcare innovation ecosystem. The partnership with Handong creates value across multiple layers:

- Connecting university R&D assets with startups
- Leveraging a regional hospital network with deep clinical and regulatory expertise
- Utilizing Pohang TechnoPark's infrastructure support

## What's next

The new branch will focus on early-stage bio & healthcare startups — offering capital, mentorship, and physical space, while acting as a bridge to Seoul and the global SparkLabs network.
