## How capital flows in the AI era

As the AI revolution accelerates, startup financing is being rewritten. From our seat as early investors, three shifts stand out.

### 1. Capital concentrates around AI-native founders

Founders who treat LLMs, AI agents, and vertical AI as core competencies from day one — not as features bolted on later — are rising to the top of capital allocation priorities. "AI-enabled" products are being outcompeted by "AI-impossible-without" products.

### 2. The 18-month runway dividing line

Through a higher rate and tighter capital environment, the principles we emphasize to our portfolio are simple:

- **Maintain 18+ months of runway**
- **Minimize fixed costs, maximize variable costs**
- **Drive unit economics through customer-level profitability**

### 3. Timing your global move

Founders who design for global markets from day one — not as an afterthought after "proving it in Korea" — consistently command better valuations and access better investor pools.
