SparkLabs and SparkLabs Saudi Arabia are co-operating **Tech Founders in Korea 2025** with Saudi Arabia's Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (**MCIT**).

## Program overview

- **Who**: Growth-stage tech startups from the MENA region
- **When**: Second half of 2025, Seoul (exact dates to be announced)
- **What you get**: Matching with Korean partners and customers, access to the SparkLabs network, and consideration for Seed / Series A investment

## How to apply

Applications are handled through official SparkLabs Saudi Arabia channels. More details will follow.
